This web application is developed to promote TRANSPARENCY and ACCOUNTABILITY in managing the student fund within the school community.

It provides an organized way to RECORD, TRACK, and MONITOR PAYMENTS contributed by members. By keeping records visible and easy to follow, this tool helps build trust, ensures fairness, and reduces the risk of errors.

Designed with simplicity in mind, it ensures that STUDENTS, GROUP MEMBERS, and GUARDIANS can access real-time financial updates with ease.
