üöÄ Getting Started

1. Sign In: Click "Sign in with Google" to authenticate using your Google account. This is required to create and manage payment tabs.

2. Create a Tab: Click the blue "+" button at the top to create a new payment tracking tab. Give it a meaningful name (e.g., "Class Fund 2024").

3. Add Members: Use the "Add Member" button in the payment form to add group members who will be making payments.

4. Record Payments: Select a member from the dropdown, enter the payment amount, and click "Add Payment" to record a transaction.

5. Track Progress: View the "Whole Progress Overview" table to see each member's payment status and the overall collection progress.

üìä Key Features

Payment Summary: View total collected, outstanding amounts, and completion percentage in the summary cards and pie chart.

Payment History: See a complete chronological record of all payments with dates, times, and amounts. You can delete incorrect entries.

Quick Info: Add custom notes, deadlines, or important information using the Quick Info section (click the "+" button).

Search by UID: Each tab has a unique identifier (UID). Copy and share it with others to let them view payment progress in read-only mode.

Export to PDF: Generate professional landscape PDF reports of payment data for record-keeping or sharing.

Multiple Tabs: Create up to 5 tabs to track different groups, events, or payment collections simultaneously.

üîç Using the Search Feature

1. Go to the Landing Page and find the "Search Payment Records" section

2. Enter the UID (unique identifier) of the tab you want to view

3. View detailed payment information including member progress, payment history, and summary statistics

4. Export the results as a PDF for offline access or sharing

Note: Anyone with a tab's UID can view its data. Only share UIDs with trusted individuals.

Edit Member Names: Click on a member's name in the overview table to edit it.

Delete Payments: In the Payment History section, click the delete button next to any payment to remove it.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my payment data secure?
A: Yes, data is stored in Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. Only you and people with your UID can access your tabs.

Q: Does GPTracker process actual payments?
A: No, GPTracker is a record-keeping tool only. It does not handle money transfers or financial transactions.

Q: Can I use GPTracker offline?
A: No, GPTracker requires an internet connection to sync data with Firebase.

Q: How many members can I add?
A: There is no strict limit, but we recommend keeping groups manageable (under 100 members) for optimal performance.

Q: Can I recover deleted data?
A: No, deleted tabs and payments are permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Please be careful when deleting.

üêõ Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Unable to sign in: Ensure your Google account is active, pop-ups are enabled, and you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
  • Payments not showing: Refresh the page (F5) or click the refresh icon. Check that you're viewing the correct tab.
  • Incorrect member totals: Verify all entries in Payment History. Delete and re-add any incorrect payments.
  • Tab not loading: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page. Check your internet connection.
  • Export not working: Ensure pop-ups and downloads are enabled in your browser. Try a different browser if issues persist.
  • UID search returns no results: Double-check the UID for typos. Ensure the tab creator hasn't deleted the tab.
  • Chart not displaying: Refresh the page. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

üí° Tips for Best Results

  • Use descriptive tab names to easily identify different payment groups
  • Record payments promptly to keep data up-to-date
  • Regularly export PDFs as backups of your payment records
  • Use Quick Info to note payment deadlines, bank details, or collection purposes
  • Share your UID only with trusted group members
  • Verify payment amounts before recording to avoid errors
  • Keep your Google account secure to protect your payment data
